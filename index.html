John Romberg - Romeo and Juliet Project (May 2025)

This is my English 9 Romeo and Juliet Project, written in May of 2025. My chosen project is the "Jams" option, presented in the form of a website, as a digital program for an unconventional symphony concert, featuring 18 pieces and movements of pieces, chosen to represent different scenes from Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. The music page includes the titles and corresponding scenes from the play, with links to the explanations or context sentences, as well as a Spotify playlist with recordings of every piece. The context page includes my 1-2 sentence explanations of the context for each of 14 pieces, and the explanations page includes more in-depth explanations for each of the four remaining pieces.
